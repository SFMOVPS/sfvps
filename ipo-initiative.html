Interrupt, Predict, and Organize

Domestic violence and street violence negatively affect not only those directly involved, but also the larger San Francisco community. In 2012, Mayor Edwin Lee launched the Interrupt, Predict and Organize (IPO) to reduce domestic and street violence in San Francisco. The initiative guides much of the work of the Office of Violence Prevention Services does.
