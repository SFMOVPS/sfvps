Direct victims are individuals who are harmed or injured as a result of a crime. Indirect victims include families, friends, and witnesses to crime. The team connects victims to services such as legal, financial, relocation, and mental health support services. Under the leadership of Mayor Edwin Lee, San Francisco experienced a reduction in street violence with a 36% in homicides; however, our work is not complete until we ensure the wellbeing of communities, families, and children across San Francisco.
